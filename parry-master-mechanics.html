Parry Master Fight System

"Hark, and fix this in thy soul: until the Six Sacred Tenets of War are mastered, thy path shall remain forever barren."
-- Master

1. Defending:
  • Hold block to defend against your opponent's attacks. This will consume your guard bar.
  • If your guard bar is depleted, you'll be unable to block.
2. Basic attack:
  • Press any direction to perform the basic attack.
  • Your opponent will block your attack unless you hit the unprotected area.
  • Basic attacks don't inflict significant damage, but they fill your special bar.
  • The special bar is reset to zero when you are blocked or hit.
3. Special attack:
  • Land three successful basic attacks to earn one special attack.
  • You need a full special bar to perform a special attack.
  • To perform a special attack, hold block and press any direction.
  • You can have a maximum of 3 special attacks at any given time.
4. Super attack:
  • You earn a super attack when you fill 3 special bars.
  • To perform a super attack, hold the special button and press any direction.
  • You can only have one super attack at any given time.
5. Parry and Counter attack:
  • To parry, press block just before an attack hits.
  • A successful parry will restore a point to your guard bar and stun the opponent.
  • Any attack on a stunned opponent turns into a counter attack and inflicts extra damage.
6. Player VS Opponent Attack:
  • The player's basic and special attacks only interrupt the opponent's basic attack.
  • The player's super attack interrupts both the opponent's basic and special attacks.
  • The opponent's super attack cannot be interrupted; it can only be blocked or parried.
