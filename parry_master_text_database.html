-- Defending: --
  1. Hold <block> to defend against your opponent's attacks. Each successful block consumes your guard bar;
  2. If your guard bar is depleted, you'll be unable to block;
  3. To replenish the guard bar, avoid using <block> for a few seconds;
  4. Block three times to continue. ()()()
-- Basic attack: --
  1. You can attack in four directions using <↑>, <↓>, <→> and <←>;
  2. Attack in all directions to continue. ()()()()
  3. Your opponent will block your attack unless you hit their unprotected area;
    Tip: look at your opponent's weapon to see what direction they are protecting.
  4. Hit you opponent's unprotected area to continue. ()()()()
  5. Your opponent might occasionaly lower their guard (idle). In that case, you can't be sure whether they'll block your attack or not;
  6. Attack your opponent when they lower their guard. ()()()
-- Special attack: --
  1. Basic attacks inflict a small amount of damage but they fill your special bar;
  2. The special bar is reset to zero when you are blocked or if you get hit;
  3. Fill up your special bar by succesfully hitting your opponent. ()()()
  4. To perform a special attack, hold <block> and press any direction;
  5. You can have a maximum of 3 special attacks at any given time;
  6. Perform a special attack. ()
-- Super attack --
  1. You can perform a super attack when you fill 3 special bars;
  2. To perform a special attack, hold <special> and press any direction;
  3. You can only have one special attack at any given time;
  4. Perform a super attack. ()
-- Parry and Counter-attack --
  1. To parry, press <block> right before an attack lands;
  2. Parrying does not consume your guard bar;
  3. Perform three parries. ()()()
  4. A successful parry will stun the opponent if their posture bar is depleted.
  5. To damage your opponent's posture bar, you need to successfully land attacks, blocks, and parries;
  6. Deplete your opponent posture bar. ()
  7. When stunned, your opponent is open for a counter-attack, which inflicts extra damage. Important: When counter-attacking, match the direction of your counter-attack to the direction of the attack you parried;
  8. Perform three counter-attacks. ()()()
  9. Be alert, your opponent can also parry your attacks and counter-attacks! You should block as soon as you are parried.
  10. Block your opponent's counter-attack. You should attack first to prompt their parry. ()
